Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Last updated: June 2026

01

Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate Spendly. This includes your name and email address when you register, and the expense records you choose to create — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or sensitive financial credentials of any kind.

02

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the features of Spendly — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and powering filters. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app. We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

03

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

04

Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typography assets. This means your browser makes a request to Google's servers when loading the app. Beyond this, we do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. Any future integrations will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy.

05

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may also request an export of your expense data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below and we will respond within 5 business days.

06

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information on our website. We are committed to resolving any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
